Multi-User Access









Our system supports role-based access for different types of users: administrators, teachers, and students. Administrators can manage schedules and invigilation duties, teachers can view and update relevant schedules, and students can access their exam timetables and receive notifications.

This feature ensures secure and tailored access, allowing each user to interact with the system based on their role, enhancing efficiency and security across the platform.
